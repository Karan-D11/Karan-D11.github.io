Revenue Breakdown
  • Roblox's revenue comes from multiple sources, primarily through in-game purchases, subscriptions, and advertising. In 2024, the company generated $3.6 billion, marking a 28.7% increase from the previous year.
  • The platform's main revenue driver is Robux sales, its virtual currency, which fuels purchases of in-game items and experiences.
  • Roblox also paid out $923 million to developers in 2024, empowering its creator community.
  • Other income streams include Premium subscriptions, ad placements, and brand partnerships, ensuring diversified growth.

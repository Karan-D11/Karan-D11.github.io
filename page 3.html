1. Dress To Impress

"Dress To Impress" is an engaging game attracting both kids and adults. With over 1.1 million players, it offers creative fun—like designing outfits or competing in fashion challenges. It encourages self-expression and social interaction, allowing players to share styles and collaborate. Multiplayer features and community-driven gameplay make it highly popular and socially rewarding.

2. Adopt Me

"Adopt Me" is a fan favorite among players aged 7 to 15, with nearly 900,000 players. It’s about adopting pets, customizing homes, and exploring virtual worlds. Its charm lies in trading, decorating, and creating stories with friends. The social and creative nature of the game has made it one of Roblox’s biggest hits.

3. Brookhaven RP

"Brookhaven RP" is a free-form role-playing game with around 850,000 active players. It supports full creativity—be a cop, a thief, or just live daily life. Players build homes, drive cars, and invent scenarios. Its simplicity and freedom make it ideal for players of all ages who want social storytelling without complex rules.
